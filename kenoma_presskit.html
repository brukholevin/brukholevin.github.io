About Kenoma

Kenoma: Action Without Action is a graphic adventure/interactive fiction game in
which you travel two paralell worlds and impose death or eternal life upon everything. Your choice.

Built of big ideas from top to bottom and posessed of
a humor that keeps things grounded, Kenoma walks the tightrope between "pretentious" and "fun" well enough to earn a
gold medal.

Combine this with a structure that begs for repeat playthroughs, a keen eye for clever puzzles, and a deep understanding of what makes a setting compelling, and you have something truly special.

English and Simplified Chinese languages supported.

Rundown

  • graphic adventure / interactive fiction hybrid

  • Core gimmick: switching between two worlds

  • One world must be saved and the other destroyed. Which gets what is decided by you

  • World of deep mysticism and fathomless depths

  • Interact with a cast of fully-realized characters, many of which have their own goals and paths

  • Major themes of existentialism, mortality, religion, and power

  • Classic 'adventure game' puzzles and exploration are accented by small 'diorama' setpieces

  • Project of solo developer "Jack Levin"

About BrukhoLevin

BrukhoLevin consists of owner/proprietor Jack Levin, and no one else. My motto is simple: Make art for freaks.

I've been a full-fledged artist in various mediums for six years; including music, visual art, writing, and game development. I'm classically trained in programming, and have been doing that for eight years. I am very passionate about games as an artistic medium, and hope to provide novel and fun experiences while keeping things relatively highbrow.
