Principessa della magia del sangue




In the aftermath of family tragedy, in a town that is no longer your home, there are still videos to rent.

"Principessa della magia del sangue" tours a fictional slice of small-town Illinois as you seek the titular film. Of course, nothing can be so simple.

This game was created ground-up in about a month for the Emotionally Loaded Jam #2.

Download also includes an exclusive PNG image and the game's OST.
