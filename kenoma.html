The world has been in two pieces since time immemorial. One knows death, the other does not. Which would you rather live in?

Take the role of a determined but aimless wanderer, and decide for yourself.

  • 2D-sidescrolling graphic adventure game
  • Explore an ancient temple split into two overlapping worlds with puzzles based on this distinction.
  • Meet strange characters with compelling personalities and their own things to do.
  • Choose to accept or deny entropy over the course of your journey.
